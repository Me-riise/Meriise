About ME-RIISE

Malnad Enclave for Research, Innovation, Incubation, Start-ups and Entrepreneurship (ME-RIISE) was established by Malnad College of Engineering (MCE) in 2018 with the support of the management, teaching staff, and students.

Over the years, ME-RIISE has organized more than 100 events and initiatives, spearheaded by Dr. Geetha Kiran A, benefiting over 3000 students.

Key Achievements:
Supported the development of eight innovative prototypes.
Contributed to the creation of nine new applications.

Building on its success, ME-RIISE has taken a significant step by forming a distinct entity, ME-RIISE FOUNDATION, a Section 8 company, in September 2022. Promoted by Malnad College of Engineering, this foundation aims to expand its reach and impact.

ME-RIISE FOUNDATION at a Glance:
Comprises 5 Directors and 2 Advisors.
Dedicated to fostering innovation, incubation, start-ups, and entrepreneurship.
Focused on empowering students, youth, and aspiring entrepreneurs to unlock their potential and contribute to economic growth.

The foundation is committed to continuing the legacy of ME-RIISE by promoting cutting-edge activities in collaboration with Malnad College of Engineering to nurture innovation and entrepreneurial spirit in the region.


Vision

To engender industry ready graduates possessing magnificent skills in the arena of innovation and technological development of products/ services.
